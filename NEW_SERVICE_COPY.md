## Lead Magnet: Body Operating System Checklist

### Page Copy

**Headline:**
The Body Operating System Checklist

**Subheadline:**
A 10-item self-assessment to evaluate how well your body's systems are functioning. Get clarity on what's working, what's not, and what needs upgrading.

### Checklist Items (10 Yes/No Questions)

1. I can stand with good alignment (ears over shoulders, shoulders over hips, hips over ankles) without effort
2. I can take a full, deep breath without restriction or tension
3. I can move through full range of motion in all major joints without pain or restriction
4. I have adequate strength for daily activities and my chosen activities
5. I can balance on one leg for 30 seconds without wobbling
6. I am free from chronic pain, tension, or discomfort
7. I move smoothly and efficiently without compensation patterns
8. I recover well from activity and don't feel stiff or sore for days
9. I can maintain good posture throughout the day without fatigue
10. My movements feel coordinated and integrated, not disconnected or awkward
