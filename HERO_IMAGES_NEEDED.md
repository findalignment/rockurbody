# Hero Images Needed for Rock Your Body

This document lists all hero images needed for the site with specific filenames and content suggestions.

## Format Requirements
- **Dimensions:** 1920px × 1080px minimum (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **File formats:** Both JPG and WebP versions needed
- **Naming convention:** `filename-hero.jpg` and `filename-hero.webp`
- **Location:** `/public/` folder (root)
- **Image quality:** High-resolution, professional, relevant to page content

## Image Naming Convention for Airtable

In your Airtable "Site Images" table, the **Name** field should be **exactly** as shown above (without the .jpg extension):

Examples:
- `gravity-structural-integration-hero`
- `exercise-for-life-hero`
- `surfers-hero`
- `back-pain-hero`

The script will automatically add the correct file extension based on what you upload.
